Web Site Portfolio

Goals and Objectives

The purpose of this project is to establish an online presence and showcase a series of design and development projects. The project consisted of a dual approach that incorporated design decisions such as layout, color composition, and typography in addition to development decisions such as front-end framework and custom CSS styles.
